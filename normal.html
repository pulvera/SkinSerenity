Normal Skin Skincare Routine



Day:

1. Wash your face with a gentle cleanser to remove dirt, oil, and sweat.

2. Apply a toner to remove any leftover impurities and prep your skin for the next steps.

3. Apply a serum to target specific concerns, such as wrinkles or uneven skin tone.

4. Apply a moisturizer to hydrate your skin and protect it from the elements.

5. Apply sunscreen with SPF 30 or higher to protect your skin from sun damage.


Night:

1. Use a makeup remover to remove all traces of makeup.

2. Wash your face with a gentle cleanser to remove dirt, oil, and sweat.

3. Apply a toner to remove any leftover impurities and prep your skin for the next steps.

4. Apply a serum to target specific concerns, such as wrinkles or uneven skin tone.

5. Apply a night cream to hydrate your skin and help it repair itself while you sleep.
